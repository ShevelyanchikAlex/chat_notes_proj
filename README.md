# chat_notes_proj

This is an application for comfortable taking notes.

## Main functions

- the ability to create folders with subject notes;
- the ability to take new notes in folders;
- for convenience, you can select categories for notes;
- the ability to add notes to favorites;
- the ability to add folders to favorites;
- the ability to add tags;
- the ability to search notes by words, favorites, tags;
- the ability to keep track of all the notes on the timeline screen;
- the ability to search notes by words, favorites, tags and folders on the timeline screen;
- the ability to monitor the general statistics on the summary screen.

## Additional functions

- the ability to add your categories;
- the ability to change various appearance settings;
- the ability to share the application on social networks;
- the ability to change the time when creating a note;
- the ability to change font size;
- the ability to change bubble alignment;
- the ability to reset all settings.
